# 格爾諾宇宙 行為準則
# Gerlno-Universe Code of Conduct

## 我們的承諾
身為格爾諾宇宙（Gerlno-Universe）社區的成員、貢獻者與管理者，我們承諾讓所有參與者皆能享有無騷擾的體驗，不受年齡、體型、顯性或隱性殘疾、族裔、生理特徵、性別認同與表達、經驗層級、教育背景、社會經濟地位、國籍、個人外貌、種族、宗教信仰或性傾向影響。
我們承諾以開放、友善、多元、包容且健康的方式互動與行動，共建良好的社區環境。

### Our Pledge
We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our Gerlno-Universe community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.
We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## 我們的行為標準
能為格爾諾宇宙（Gerlno-Universe）社區營造正向環境的行為包括：
* 對他人展現同理與善意
* 尊重不同的觀點、看法與經歷
* 給予並坦然接受建設性的回饋
* 為自身錯誤承擔責任，向受影響者致歉並從中學習
* 不僅考量個人利益，更以社區整體福祉為重

不可接受的行為包括：
* 使用帶有性意味的語言或圖像，以及任何形式的性關注或追求
* 惡意挑釁、發表侮辱或貶損言論，並進行人身或政治攻擊
* 公開或私下的騷擾行為
* 未經他人明確同意，公開其私人資訊（如實際地址、電子郵箱等）
* 其他在專業場域中被合理認定為不適當的行為

### Our Standards
Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment for our Gerlno-Universe community include:
* Demonstrating empathy and kindness toward other people
* Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
* Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
* Accepting responsibility and apologizing to those affected by our mistakes, and learning from the experience
* Focusing on what is best not just for us as individuals, but for the overall community

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:
* The use of sexualized language or imagery, and sexual attention or advances of any kind
* Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks
* Public or private harassment
* Publishing others' private information, such as a physical or email address, without their explicit permission
* Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## 執行責任
社區管理者負責釐清並執行格爾諾宇宙（Gerlno-Universe）社區的可接受行為標準，並針對任何不適當、具威脅性、冒犯性或有害的行為，採取適當且公平的矯正措施。
社區管理者有權且有責任移除、編輯或拒絕任何不符合本行為準則的評論、代碼提交、維基編輯、議題及其他貢獻內容，並於適當時說明審核決定的理由。

### Enforcement Responsibilities
Community leaders are responsible for clarifying and enforcing our standards of acceptable behavior in the Gerlno-Universe community and will take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any behavior that they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.
Community leaders have the right and responsibility to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, wiki edits, issues, and other contributions that are not aligned to this Code of Conduct, and will communicate reasons for moderation decisions when appropriate.

## 適用範圍
本行為準則適用於格爾諾宇宙（Gerlno-Universe）的所有社區空間，包含GitHub代碼倉庫（https://github.com/GERLNO/Gerlno-Universe）與官方網站（https://gerlno.github.io/Gerlno-Universe/）；同時適用於個人以社區官方身份在公開場合的所有行為。
代表本社區的行為示例包括：使用社區官方電子郵箱、透過官方社交媒體帳號發布內容、在線上或線下活動中擔任指定代表。

### Scope
This Code of Conduct applies within all community spaces of Gerlno-Universe, including the GitHub repository (https://github.com/GERLNO/Gerlno-Universe) and the official website (https://gerlno.github.io/Gerlno-Universe/), and also applies when an individual is officially representing the community in public spaces.
Examples of representing our community include using an official e-mail address, posting via an official social media account, or acting as an appointed representative at an online or offline event.

## 違規舉報
任何虐待、騷擾或其他不可接受的行為，均可向負責執行本準則的社區管理者舉報，聯繫郵箱：GERLNO@outlook.com。
所有投訴將被及時、公正地審查與調查，社區管理者均有義務嚴格保護舉報人的隱私與安全。

### Enforcement
Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the community leaders responsible for enforcement at GERLNO@outlook.com.
All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
All community leaders are obligated to respect the privacy and security of the reporter of any incident.

## 執行準則
社區管理者將依據以下社區影響評估準則，決定對違反本行為準則之行為的處置方式：

### 1. 糾正
**社區影響**：使用不適當語言，或其他被認定為不專業、不受社區歡迎的行為。
**處置結果**：社區管理者發出私人書面警告，明確說明違規性質及行為不適當的原因，並可能要求違規者公開致歉。

#### 1. Correction
**Community Impact**: Use of inappropriate language or other behavior deemed unprofessional or unwelcome in the community.
**Consequence**: A private, written warning from community leaders, providing clarity around the nature of the violation and an explanation of why the behavior was inappropriate. A public apology may be requested.

### 2. 警告
**社區影響**：透過單次事件或一連串行為違反行為標準。
**處置結果**：發出警告並說明持續違規的後果，指定期限內禁止與相關人員互動（包括主動接觸執行本準則的管理者），此限制涵蓋社區空間及社交媒體等外部渠道，違反該限制可能導致臨時或永久封禁。

#### 2. Warning
**Community Impact**: A violation through a single incident or series of actions.
**Consequence**: A warning with consequences for continued behavior. No interaction with the people involved, including unsolicited interaction with those enforcing the Code of Conduct, for a specified period of time. This includes avoiding interactions in community spaces as well as external channels like social media. Violating these terms may lead to a temporary or permanent ban.

### 3. 臨時封禁
**社區影響**：嚴重違反社區標準，包含持續的不適當行為。
**處置結果**：指定期限內，臨時禁止參與社區任何形式的互動或公開溝通，期間不得與相關人員進行公開或私人互動（包括主動接觸執行本準則的管理者），違反該限制將直接導致永久封禁。

#### 3. Temporary Ban
**Community Impact**: A serious violation of community standards, including sustained inappropriate behavior.
**Consequence**: A temporary ban from any sort of interaction or public communication with the community for a specified period of time. No public or private interaction with the people involved, including unsolicited interaction with those enforcing the Code of Conduct, is allowed during this period. Violating these terms may lead to a permanent ban.

### 4. 永久封禁
**社區影響**：出現重複違反社區標準的行為模式，包含持續的不適當行為、單獨騷擾個人，或針對特定群體的攻擊、貶損行為。
**處置結果**：永久禁止參與本社區任何形式的公開互動。

#### 4. Permanent Ban
**Community Impact**: Demonstrating a pattern of violation of community standards, including sustained inappropriate behavior, harassment of an individual, or aggression toward or disparagement of classes of individuals.
**Consequence**: A permanent ban from any sort of public interaction within the community.
